Steel is our skin.

Our product culture is born from a solid structural soul. An exclusive premise for construction entirely in steel makes our steel kitchens unique. Steel is our skin, a noble material, unalterably hygienic. We have inherited the product culture of top professional restaurants, and we believe in hidden qualities.As with the best raw materials, all the true quality and original flavour hides within an ingredient.

A tailor made suit.

A kitchen is to be experienced. Xera is a partner in design. Like a tailor's workshop, the steel kitchen take shape from a personal vision of the customers and of their needs. The process for the realisation of a creation is the result of a participatory project between the designer and our technical staff. Cooking is a complex activity that cannot be standardised, either in the functions or in the aesthetics. We want to privilege the quality of the process and not only of the product.

Go Green

xera Kitchens is well aware of its environmental responsibility. For this reason, we use Stainless Steel. Front nishes or doors are made in waterproof hdhmr,stone , Acrylic or Tempered Glass. We only use wood grown with sustainable forest management. All the paints and lacquers used in production are solvent-free. xera Kitchens is always looking for environmentally beneficial materials and sustainable manufacturing method to include our production process thus to full our responsibility to the future generations. Xera kitchens with European or US standards are tailor-made for Indian cooking. xera kitchens are easy to install at site

The basis of our domestic kitchens is totally built in stainless steel. With us there are no dimensional constraints, our items can be custom made according to your project.
