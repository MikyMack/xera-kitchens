04 Go Green

Environmental Responsibility

Xera Kitchens is well aware of its environmental responsibility. For this reason, we use Stainless Steel. Front finishes or doors are made in waterproof HDHMR, stone, Acrylic or Tempered Glass. We only use wood grown with sustainable forest management. All the paints and lacquers used in production are solvent-free. Xera Kitchens is always looking for environmentally beneficial materials and sustainable manufacturing methods to include in our production process thus to fulfill our responsibility to future generations. Xera kitchens with European or US standards are tailor-made for Indian cooking. Xera kitchens are easy to install at site.
